Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Joinzo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your data when you use the Joinzo mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect precise location data to verify you are within a 10-minute delivery zone and to locate the nearest dark store.
  • Contacts: With your permission, we access your contacts to allow you to invite neighbors to "Joinzo Loops" for community discounts.
  • Account Info: We store your email and verified phone number for authentication via Supabase.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data solely to provide delivery services and social shopping features. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Security

Your data is protected using Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) and industry-standard encryption.

4. Contact Us

For questions regarding this policy, contact support@joinzo.example.com
